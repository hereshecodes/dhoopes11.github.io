If you live in America, your rights to privacy get less and less all the time, ESPECIALLY as an individual with a uterus

Here are some actionable steps for protecting yourself and information about who collects, who buys and who sells your data


Selling data is incredibly lucrative. In the U.S. alone there are many data brokers (Companies who buy, sell, and license data) who openly advertise the data of people living in the U.S.

Legislators in states like California have done more to try and protect information of their constituents through CCPA CPRA (Effective January 1, 2023)
