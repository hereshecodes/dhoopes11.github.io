About

My path to full-stack software development.

  • 2014-2018

    B.S. Computer Science - SNHU

    As the first female graduate of the Computer Science distance learning program at Southern New Hampshire University, I learned a great deal about managing my time effectively, and maintaining integrity out of the physical classroom.

  • 2015-2018

    Goodyear Systems Support Arkansas

    While working on my B.S., I worked full-time at the Goodyear support desk helping business users nationwide navigate our systems. I wrote technical support documentation and worked with senior developers to begin learning our software development cycle in preparation for graduation, and a role on the software team.

  • January 2018

    Software Developer Goodyear, Arkansas

    After successfully writing and implementing the current support ticket system, I was promoted to Software Development Analyst and began working to convert our Classic ASP systems to ASP.NET MVC Azure applications.

  • January 2020

    Mobile Applications Developer Mascot Media, Arkansas

    Design, develop, and maintain the web-based applications and systems using various programming languages and tools.
